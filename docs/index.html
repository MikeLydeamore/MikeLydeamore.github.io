Dr Michael Lydeamore

Twitter LinkedIn GitHub Scholar Monash

I am a lecturer in Business Analytics and Data Science in the Department of Econometrics and Business Statistics at Monash University. I also hold honorary appointments with Alfred Health and SaferCare Victoria.

I love working on translational and high-impact projects across any industry. If you have a question or a project that you think needs a data-driven approach, please get in touch with the details below.

My research interests are:

  • Health analytics,
  • Healthcare acquired infections,
  • Understanding data collection methods in the healthcare setting,
  • Infectious diseases control,
  • Statistical software development and workflow.

I am an advocate of re-use of historical datasets, and performing analysis in as open of a way as possible while preserving privacy of those whom data has been collected. I have an active GitHub profile which contains most of my research code that can be made publicly available.

I thoroughly enjoy engagement with societies and research centres. I am currently the Treasurer of the Australia and New Zealand Industrial and Applied Mathematics (ANZIAM) society, ECA Chair for the NHRMC Centre of Research Excellence SPECTRUM, and in a non-academic context, treasurer of the North Park Tennis Club.
